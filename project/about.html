Our Story

Founded in 2025, our mission is to ensure every child has access to warm clothing, no matter their circumstances. We work tirelessly with volunteers and donors to collect and distribute clothes that bring dignity and warmth.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no child feels the cold of poverty, and every child can face the future with confidence and hope.
